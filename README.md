# Agonis

**Agonis** is a social network for gamers — think *Letterboxd meets Facebook*, built for tracking, rating, and talking about the games you play. Search a massive game database, log your library, write reviews, follow friends, and share what you're playing in a real-time social feed.

---

## Features

### Track & Rate
- **IGDB-powered search** — a massive, cached game database with cover art, summaries, and platform info.
- **Library management** — organize games into *Backlog, Playing, Completed, Dropped,* and *100% Completed*.
- **Ratings & reviews** — rate out of 5 stars, write personal notes, and log completion dates.
- **Masterpieces showcase** — your 5-star games are automatically featured on your profile.
- **Steam import** *(Premium)* — pull your owned games straight from your Steam library.
- **Custom lists** — curate themed collections like "My Top 10 RPGs".

### Social
- **Live feed** — post updates, tag games, attach screenshots, and mark spoilers.
- **Comments & likes** — with `@mentions` and real-time notifications.
- **Follow system** — public and private profiles, with follow requests for private accounts.
- **Shareable cards** — export review and post cards as images for sharing off-platform.

### Profiles & Insights
- **Personal dashboard** — total games, completion rate, average rating, achievement badges.
- **Advanced insights** *(Premium)* — rating distribution, platform breakdown, and monthly activity charts.
- **Custom accent color** *(Premium)* — personalize your profile and username color.
- **Privacy controls** — toggle your library and profile between public and private.

### Trust & Safety
- **Blocking & reporting** — block abusive accounts and report posts or users.
- **Bot protection** — hCaptcha-gated authentication.
- **Data portability** — export your full account data as JSON at any time.
- **Account deletion** — permanently delete your account and all associated data, on request.

### Platform
- **Installable PWA** — works offline-first and installs like a native app.
- **Light & dark mode**, fully responsive.
- **Premium** — unlocked with a one-time, non-recurring payment via Ko-fi.
